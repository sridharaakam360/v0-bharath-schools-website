Admission Process

Step 1

Submit Application

Fill out the online application form or collect from the school office

Step 2

Entrance Test

Appear for the entrance assessment as per the scheduled date

Step 3

Interview

Parent and student interaction with the admission committee

Step 4

Enrollment

Complete fee payment and document submission for final enrollment

Admission Enquiry

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (Original & Copy)
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • Report Card / Mark Sheet of last class
  • Passport size photographs (6 nos.)
  • Aadhaar Card of Student
  • Aadhaar Card of Parents
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill/Ration Card)
  • Parent's ID Proof

Download Application Form

Download the admission application form, fill it completely, and submit it at the school office along with required documents.
